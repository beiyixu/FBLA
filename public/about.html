Hey Wolves! FBLA (Future Business Leaders Of America) is a club for everyone, even if you aren't pursuing a business career. It is a way in which you explore and delve into the business world by competing in events (accounting, business math, global businesses, hospitality management, sports entertainment, digital video production to name a few) for districts, state, and nationals. FBLA is also a club set out to make a DIFFERENCE in the Grandview community with charity events and designing spirit wear, but it is also meant to bring people together with bonding events and having a whole lot of fun (movie nights, laser tag, selfies).
