GHS Craft Fair

We're BACK!!! The Grandview High School Craft Fair will be back this year so Mark Your Calendars and SAVE THE DATE!! Come shop with us and our amazing vendors on Saturday, December 4th, 2021.

The annual Craft Fair held at Grandview High School has been a beloved tradition that has allowed local vendors to advertise their business and sell products to the community during the holiday season. This is a great opportunity to get your business and/or products to the community, as this is one of the biggest craft fairs in Arapahoe County. In addition to going out into the community and growing as a local business, the Craft Fair also provides opportunities for Grandview High School students to gain more knowledge real world experience through clubs such as Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA). This year, in a partnership with Grandview PTCO, the Grandview FBLA chapter is managing the 2021 Craft Fair. FBLA is a club that helps students grow in their business knowledge and skills by participating in competitions around the country. Through the Craft Fair, the FBLA chapter at Grandview High School will be raising money to help offset their travel costs for their current- and future- state and national qualifiers. By participating in this wonderful opportunity, you will be helping not only your business, but you will be enriching the community and the futures of these bright students. The FBLA Craft Fair committee is working tirelessly to make this experience a success, not only for their club, but for local businesses and the community. The craft fair is a positive community experience that now is truly providing an opportunity for all business leaders, present and future, to succeed.

Weather Notice

If there is a storm or other severe weather, the decision whether or not to hold the fair is up to the school administration, not the Craft Fair organizers. If the district decides to close its buildings, all activities are automatically cancelled. This decision is made (even on Saturdays) by 5:00 am. Please visit www.cherrycreekschools.org for cancellation information. District weather cancellations are also broadcast over major radio stations. If you hear that “Cherry Creek School District” is closed for December 7, you’ll know the Craft Fair is cancelled. We are sorry, but if that happens we will not be able to reschedule for an alternate date, nor can we offer refunds. Let’s hope for good weather.
